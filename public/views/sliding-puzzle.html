Sliding puzzle

Game description

A sliding puzzle puzzle that challenges a player to slide pieces along certain routes on a board to establish a certain end-configuration. The pieces to be moved will consist of simple rectagles and will be imprinted with numbers and sections of a larger picture.

How to play

Drag and drop the desired piece in the adjacent empty space.
